Privacy Policy

Effective: May 19, 2026

TroyStack is a precious metals portfolio tracking service operated by Mancini Tech Solutions LLC. We track what you tell us about your holdings, we use AI to help you understand markets and process receipts, and we use third-party services to deliver that experience. This document tells you what data we collect, who we share it with, and what your rights are. We've tried to write it in plain English.

1. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Hashed password (managed by Supabase Auth, never visible to us)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Subscription tier and purchase history (managed by Apple In-App Purchase, RevenueCat, and Stripe)

Portfolio data

  • Precious metals holdings you record (metal type, weight, cost basis, purchase date)
  • Receipt images you upload for scanning
  • Notes and tags you add to holdings

Chat and voice data (when you use Troy, our AI assistant)

  • Chat messages you send to Troy
  • Voice recordings when you use voice input
  • Voice playback requests when you use voice output

Device and technical data

  • Device push notification token (only for alerts you've subscribed to)
  • IP address (logged briefly by hosting infrastructure for standard operational purposes; not retained or used to identify you)
  • App version and operating system version

2. How we use the information

  • Provide the core service: track your portfolio, calculate value against live spot prices, generate intelligence summaries, send alerts you've opted into
  • Operate the Troy AI assistant: route your messages through third-party AI providers to generate responses
  • Process subscription payments through Apple, Stripe, and RevenueCat
  • Improve the product through aggregated, non-identifying usage patterns
  • Communicate with you about your account or the service

Receipt images you upload are processed in memory and are not written to our servers' disk storage. They are forwarded to Google Gemini for OCR processing and are not retained by us afterward.

4. What we don't do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not show advertising in the app.
  • We do not provide your portfolio data or chat content to any AI provider for the purpose of training their models. Anthropic, Google, OpenAI, ElevenLabs, and xAI each have their own published terms on training data usage. As of this writing, none of our AI providers' API terms permit training on API-submitted data by default.
  • We do not share your portfolio holdings with any party not listed in Section 3.
  • We do not access your portfolio data for marketing or sales targeting. Production data access is logged and limited to debugging and support purposes.

5. Your rights

  • Access your data: export available in app settings.
  • Delete your account: available in app settings; deletion is immediate and removes your data from our primary systems within 30 days. Some third-party providers may retain data according to their own policies (listed in Section 3).
  • Update your information: editable in app settings.
  • Contact us: support@troystack.com

6. Children's privacy

TroyStack is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. International users

We are based in the United States. By using TroyStack you consent to processing in the United States.

8. Changes to this policy

We will update the effective date when we make material changes. Significant changes will be communicated in the app.
